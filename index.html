The history about basketball

On December 21, 1891, it was invented by James Naismith, a physical education teacher at the YMCA Training School in Springfield, Massachusetts. In 1904, the first basketball exhibition game was held at the St. Louis Olympics. In 1936, basketball was listed as an official event in the Berlin Olympics
